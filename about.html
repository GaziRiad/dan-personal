ABOUT ARES

Started by Peter Bentley Brandt, Ares Art Management is a distinguished private art dealership specializing in the acquisition, sale, and consultation of high-value artworks, with a focus on modern and contemporary art. Catering to an exclusive clientele that includes seasoned collectors, emerging art enthusiasts, and institutional buyers, Peter Bentley Brandt brings his extensive expertise to every facet of the business

Expertise and Services

Peter Bentley Brandt, with his robust art market background personally oversees all operations at Ares Art Management. This includes handling private sales and purchases, as well as offering comprehensive collection management and investment advice. Known for his discretion and personalized service, Peter ensures that each client's needs and privacy are prioritized, delivering tailored solutions to each collector’s unique requirements.

Clientele and Approach

Under Peter’s direction, Ares Art Management serves a diverse, global clientele, providing services meticulously tailored to the varying tastes and needs of individual collectors and institutions. Peter specializes in sourcing rare and soughtafter artworks, leveraging his extensive network within the art world to access private collections and exclusive sales.

Collection Management and Advisory

Beyond transactions, Peter offers strategic collection management services through Ares Art Management. This includes curating collections based on thematic or aesthetic considerations, advising on potential acquisitions and deaccessions, and managing logistical details such as transportation and storage. He also provides expert valuation services, assisting clients in understanding the market value of artworks and making informed decisions based on current market trends.

Privacy and Discretion

Understanding the sensitive nature of art transactions, especially those involving significant financial commitments, Peter upholds the highest standards of confidentiality and discretion. Clients can trust him to handle their affairs with the utmost privacy, from the initial consultation to the final stages of acquisition or sale.

Conclusion

Ares Art Management, under the stewardship of Peter Bentley Brandt, is more than just a private dealership; it is a full-service art advisory firm dedicated to the nuanced needs of modern art collectors. Whether for acquisition, sale, or strategic advice, Peter offers expert service with integrity, discretion, and unmatched expertise in the art market.
